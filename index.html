About Me

Aspiring Software Engineer

Greetings! First of all, thank you for taking the time to visit my website. As you may have read above, my name is Mitchell and I'm an aspiring software engineer from Houston, Texas. I'm currently a sophomore at the University of Houston with a major in Computer Science and a minor in Mathematics, planning to graduate in May 2024.

As of writing this (in January 2022), I've just finished my sophomore-level CS classes, and I'm feeling ready to contribute what I've learned thus far to a real-world project. I'm currently working on a number of personal projects which you can read about above, or on my GitHub page.

As you may gather from my projects and working experience, I have a passion for education - especically langauge learning. Since I started self-studying Japanese as a teenager, I've developed a deep interest in linguistic education. That interest has influenced me as a programmer as well, and inspired me to create a number of language-related projects including a translator Discord bot, and a spaced repetition system.

In the future, I hope to continue working on meaningful projects to solve problems, contribute to positive causes, and help people achieve their goals.
